Get to Know Me

I am Prathap, a passionate UI/UX and digital designer focused on creating modern, user-friendly, and visually engaging digital experiences.

I design using tools like Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Blender, Canva, and Adobe XD to craft intuitive interfaces and creative visuals. I also integrate AI-powered tools like Stitch into my workflow to speed up design processes, enhance creativity, and transform designs into functional code.

My approach combines creativity, technology, and problem-solving to build responsive, impactful, and meaningful digital products. Creative Process

Our sequential workflow timeline, animated as you scroll.

01

Discover

Understanding client requirements, analyzing design space, definitions, scope, and mapping core constraints.

02

Research

Analyzing competitors, reviewing user feedback, creating user personas, and drawing visual inspirations.

03

Ideate

Sketching options, brainstorming layouts, visual storytelling flows, and conceptual mapping of assets.

04

Design

Crafting design guidelines, selecting premium typography, creating layout grids, and visual polishing in Figma or Photoshop.

05

Prototype

Linking views into high-fidelity click-through interactions in Figma, animating UI overlays, and micro-interaction checks.

06

Refine

Gathering feedback from stakeholders, running user accessibility audits, and tweaking alignments and typography weights.

07

Deliver

Packaging design assets, organizing developer hands-off folders, exporting 3D meshes, and validating implementation matches design.
